Ren Texture

Texture describes the stability of a character's Ren circulation. It determines how frequently dice trigger Boons and Complications. Triggers are per-die, based on that die's own face values.

Texture Boon Triggers Complication Triggers

Volatile Min, Min+1 Max−1, Max
Rampant Min, Min+1, Min+2 Max−2, Max−1, Max
